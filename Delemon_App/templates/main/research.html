Market Research — Delemon Technology

We combine quantitative and qualitative research to answer the questions that matter: who your best customers are (ICP), what they value, how they decide, and what will move the market. Our deliverables align with product, brand, and go-to-market teams.

From TAM/SAM/SOM sizing and pricing studies (Gabor-Granger, Van Westendorp) to concept testing, ad/creative testing, UX/usability research, and brand trackers, we turn data into clear, prioritized recommendations.

Our Market Research Services

Audience & ICP

Persona development, jobs-to-be-done, needs analysis, and journey mapping to focus positioning and messaging.

Quant Surveys

Sample design, questionnaire build, fielding, and statistical analysis to size demand and validate direction.

Interviews & FGDs

In-depth interviews and moderated focus groups to uncover motivations, barriers, and language customers use.

Task-based tests, heuristic reviews, card sorts, and click-tracking to improve conversion and retention.

Competitive & Brand

Landscape audits, share-of-voice, message testing, and brand-lift trackers to measure and improve perception.
