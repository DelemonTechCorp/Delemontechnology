FAQ

1

How many logo concepts do I receive and how are rounds handled?

1

We usually present 3–5 strategic directions. You pick a favorite and we refine it through focused rounds (fair-use unlimited tweaks). The outcome is a polished master logo with variants for every medium.

2

What file formats and guideline assets are included?

2

You’ll receive scalable vectors (AI, SVG, PDF) and web-ready PNGs, plus a brand guideline PDF covering logo usage, safe areas, colors, typography, and do’s/don’ts.

3

How fast can we launch a new logo and identity kit?

3

Typical timelines run 7–14 days depending on scope and feedback cadence. Need it sooner? Ask about our accelerated sprints for time-sensitive launches.

4

Can you adapt the logo across stationery and social profiles?

4

Yes. We design business cards, letterheads, email signatures, social avatars, cover banners, and presentation templates so you look consistent everywhere from day one.

5

Do you offer naming, tagline writing, or brand voice support?

5

Absolutely. Our strategy team can support naming frameworks, taglines, and tone-of-voice guides to align your visuals with a compelling verbal identity.
